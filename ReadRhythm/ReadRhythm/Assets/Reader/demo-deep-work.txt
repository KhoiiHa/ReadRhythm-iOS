Cal Newport argues that focused, distraction-free work is the key to producing valuable output in a noisy world. Protecting long stretches of concentration allows people to master complex tasks faster.

---

Principles

1. Work deeply by setting clear rituals and time blocks.
2. Embrace boredom so your mind can recover from intensity.
3. Quit social media or limit shallow obligations that erode focus.

---

Practice

Start with a daily 90-minute deep work block. Eliminate notifications, define a specific goal, and log your results to build momentum.
