Atomic Habits – Sample Preview

Build better habits by focusing on tiny changes that compound over time. James Clear explores how identity-based habits make lasting transformation more achievable.

---

Key Ideas

1. Make habits obvious, attractive, easy, and satisfying.
2. Stack new habits onto existing routines to reduce friction.
3. Focus on systems rather than goals to sustain progress.

---

Case Study

Clear describes a baseball coach who used small habit tweaks—like standardizing warmups and shared routines—to turn an underperforming team into consistent champions.
